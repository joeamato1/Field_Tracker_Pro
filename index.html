Burglary / Robbery / Shoplifting Policy

Burglary

  • Call 911 before entering store and follow their instructions.
  • Once cleared by police, contact supervisor who will advise you to fill out an incident report.
  • Include the police report, officer name and badge number.
  • Note suspected inventory stolen (SKUs, description, and retail value).

Robbery

  • During the robbery, stay calm and do NOT intervene.
  • Cooperate. Do not openly confront the criminal. Instruct others to cooperate as well.
  • Warn the criminal when you need to make necessary radical movements to comply with their demands.
  • Do not reveal information that isn't asked for.
  • Study the criminal's features as closely as possible (tattoos, scars, facial hair, limping, missing/gold teeth).
  • Call 911 as soon as the robber leaves.

After the Robbery

  • Note the direction of flight of the robber.
  • Write down physical description (clothing, actions, sounds, etc.).
  • Without exposing yourself to harm, get a description of the robber's vehicle.
  • Do not touch anything the robber touched (fingerprints).
  • Ask witnesses to stay or get their contact information for police.
  • Separate witnesses so they don't confuse each other.
  • Step outside when police arrive to show you are safe.
  • Do not discuss the robbery with anyone besides police or Leslie's Loss Prevention.
  • Fill out Incident Report and notify supervisor.

Shoplifting

  • Never chase a shoplifter out of your place of business.
  • Never try to physically stop or detain a shoplifter.
  • Never accuse anyone of stealing.
  • Never lock the door to keep a shoplifter from leaving.
  • Without exposing yourself to harm, get a description of the thief's vehicle.
  • Immediately call 911 and then your manager after a shoplifting incident.
  • Submit an Incident Report using AIRS.
